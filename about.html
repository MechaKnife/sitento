Meet the Founders

Dominic Christoforidis is the CEO of Sitento.

From a young age, Dominic has demonstrated a strong entrepreneurial spirit, making his first money at just 9 years old through the ownership of a Minecraft server and the sale of digital items and perks to players. He has also bought and sold game currencies in various games and provided items for people who had difficulty finding them. In addition to his entrepreneurial endeavors, Dominic has also worked a variety of regular jobs, including as a waiter and in marketing for restaurants.

In addition to his entrepreneurial and business experience, Dominic is also skilled in graphic design and has experience creating logos for individuals and companies. This creative ability, combined with his technical knowledge and business acumen, make him a well-rounded and highly qualified CEO for Sitento.

As CEO, Dominic is committed to providing the highest quality website development services to his clients, and he is dedicated to helping them achieve their goals. With his leadership and expertise, Sitento is well-positioned to become a leading provider of website development services in the industry.

In summary, Dominic Christoforidis is a highly intelligent, logical, persistent, and focused individual with a strong entrepreneurial spirit and diverse background. His experience in business, marketing, and graphic design make him an excellent choice as CEO of Sitento, and he is committed to helping his clients succeed and achieve their goals through the creation of high-quality websites. With his intelligence, logical thinking, persistence, and focus, Dominic is well-suited to lead Sitento as CEO. He has a proven track record of success in the business world and a passion for helping others, which he aims to do through the creation of high-quality websites for his clients. Dominic's diverse background and experience make him a valuable asset to the Sitento team and an excellent choice as CEO.
